About Funzone

FunZone has expanded from its location in Aptech in NorthNazimabad to
add to its 10,000 square foot indoor entertainment center and we are now
also located in Ma On Shan. Our Ma On Shan location is THE place for your kids with
5,000 square feet of indoor fun for children up to
12 years of age. Climb and slide and bump and swing — we have everything! Our playground has
been built in accordance with European Safety Standards for indoor play and materials are tested to the latest
EU flammability test. FunZone features a dedicated toddler’s area (ages 3 and under),
awesome party rooms for a birthday to remember, cafe and wifi for adults and parking is available nearby.
Our goal is to provide a safe, clean environment for parents and children
to play and exercise together…and most importantly, HAVE FUN!

Message From Funzone

A parent always wants what’s best for their child, including finding ways to
entertain, stimulate and educate them. As parents, we feel strongly that
there are not enough quality facilities and open spaces in Hong Kong for families to
play with their children and promote fun, healthy
exercise in a clean, safe environment, and so we welcome you to our custom playground along with:
Our unique climbing wall
ToddlerZone (for toddlers below 2 years of age)
FunGroups playgroup programs

At FunZone we’re committed to providing just this environment, where children can
learn the value of exercise and playing well with others on our
custom playground equipment or through our FunGroups playgroup programs.
